Manulex_infra v.2. - General description

The Manulex_Infra database provides several quantitative descriptions of the orthographic and grapho-phonological characteristics of French words encountered by children at the elementary school (Grade 1-5). The corpus of words analyzed (nearly 45,000) and the word frequencies are taken from the Manulex database. Manulex_Infra (version 1) was initially published in 2007 (reference below) and made available for download at the University of Bourgogne (currently unavailable) and for online query on the Manulex.org website. The new version (v.2) of the database, available here for download, introduces a number of major changes which are detailed under the tab 'changes to version 1'. New information is also added to facilitate the selection of words according to their grapho-phonological characteristics. An online searchable version of the database will be developed as soon as possible

The main statistical characteristics of the words analyzed in Manulex_Infra concern the relations between graphemes (G) and phonemes (Ph); the grapheme being considered as the orthographic counterpart of the phoneme (for example, 'ou' for /u/ in the word 'loup'; 'a' for /a/ in 'table'). The associations are considered in the reading direction (from grapheme to phoneme) and in the writing direction (from phoneme to grapheme). Version 2 of Manulex_Infra also introduces an analysis of grapho-phonological associations based on larger units corresponding to the phonological rime of words. Another advantage of version 2 of Manulex_Infra is to analyze the grapho-phonological relations of words not only on the basis of the lexical forms encountered in the text corpus (as in version 1), but also according to the corresponding lexemes or lemmas. This second analysis allows a description of the grapho-phonological associations by excluding the variations related to gender (masculine, feminine) and number (singular, plural) inflections, as well as to verb tenses.

A set of other characteristics are also described, such as word length indexes (letters, graphemes, syllables), word orthographic neighborhood ('n-count', Levenshtein distance), bigram and trigram frequencies, non-homographic homophones. A full description is provided under the tab 'Understanding Manulex_Infra'.

The current version of the database is v.2. Several major changes have been made to the previous version. December 2021.
